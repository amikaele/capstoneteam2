Overview

Here at APU, we have had a tradition of several years of computer science senior capstone projects centered around APU's CubeSat endeavors. The computer science senior capstone projects are the culmination of a year's worth of planning and execution geared towards developing a marketable website or application. Students are encouraged to explore building a product that interests them, and there is always a group that does work centered around APU's CubeSat program as we gear up towards a launch.The first senior project based around our program was completed during the 2020-2021 school year. This group set out to build software that would become the foundation of how intermediate or amateur radio/spacecraft enjoyers show off their data.
